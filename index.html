BEYOND THE SCREEN

Life & Inspiration,August 14, 2025

Moments that Shape Me — I find inspiration in the little things: a walk by the ocean, a book that moves me, or a conversation with someone new. Fashion is not just about style for me, it’s about expressing who I am and the journey I’m on. Every role I play and every place I visit teaches me something new about myself.

From the discipline of badminton to the artistry of cinema, my life has been a balance of focus and creativity. I carry these lessons into everything I do, whether it’s on set, at home with family, or working for causes close to my heart. This blog is where I share those stories.

Chennai Express with sarukh khan

Title description,Released: 2013

Hats! The trend this summer is hats for men!

"Hi everyone! I still remember the fun we had while shooting Chennai Express in 2013. It was such a joy to play Meenamma, and sharing the screen with Shah Rukh made every scene so memorable. I hope you enjoy watching it as much as we enjoyed making it!"

"Working on Chennai Express was an incredible journey – from vibrant South Indian locations to all the quirky, fun moments on set. Every dance, every scene had so much energy, and I loved bringing Meenamma to life. The film has a special place in my heart, and I’m thrilled that fans still celebrate its songs, colors, and laughter even today."
